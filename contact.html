What to Tell Us

The more you share, the better we can plan. Here's a simple guide:

1

Where you want to go

Or even just a feeling — "beach," "cold weather," "something cultural." We'll help with the rest.

2

When you're thinking of travelling

Rough dates are fine. We'll work around school holidays, peak seasons and your calendar.

3

Who's travelling

Solo, couple, family with kids, group of friends — it shapes everything we plan.

4

Your approximate budget

Any range is fine. We'll tell you honestly what's achievable — and what isn't.

5

Anything specific you want

A particular hotel, experience, anniversary surprise — share anything that matters.

Quick Answers

Is there a fee to get a quote?
None at all. Consultation, planning and quoting is completely free. You only pay once you decide to book.
How quickly will you respond?
WhatsApp messages typically get a response within a few hours. We're available 7 days a week.
Can I change the plan after it's submitted?
Absolutely. We iterate as many times as you need until the plan is exactly right. No pressure, no rush.
Do you handle visa assistance?
We guide you through the visa process and let you know exactly what's needed for each destination you travel to.
What if something goes wrong on the trip?
We're available 24/7 throughout your journey. If anything comes up — flight issues, hotel problems, anything — you reach us directly and we handle it.
