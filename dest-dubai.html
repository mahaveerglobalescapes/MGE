Dubai

Where the world's most audacious skyline meets ancient desert — a city that constantly surprises, and somehow always delivers.

Overview

The City That Built the Future

Dubai is unlike anywhere else on earth. It's a city that decided the impossible was merely a starting point — and then built it anyway. The Burj Khalifa. The Palm. The indoor ski slope in the desert. The souk where you can buy gold by the gram at midnight.

But Dubai also has depth beyond the spectacle. The old Al Fahidi neighbourhood. The creek at dusk. The silence of the desert an hour from downtown. We show you all of it — the dazzle and the soul.

Sample Itinerary

1

Arrival & Downtown Dubai

Airport pickup, hotel check-in. Evening at Downtown Dubai — Dubai Fountain show, Burj Khalifa at night, dinner at a lakeside restaurant.

2

Burj Khalifa & Dubai Mall

Morning visit to Burj Khalifa "At the Top." Afternoon at your leisure in the Dubai Mall. Optional aquarium and underwater zoo visit.

3

Old Dubai & Gold Souk

Al Fahidi historical neighbourhood, Dubai Museum, abra ride across the creek, spice souk and gold souk. Lunch at a traditional Emirati restaurant.

4

Desert Safari

Afternoon desert safari with dune bashing, camel ride, henna painting and sandboarding. Bedouin camp dinner with live entertainment under the stars.

5

Palm Jumeirah & Atlantis

Morning at Palm Jumeirah — the monorail, beach, optional Aquaventure waterpark. Sunset from the top of the Palm. Farewell rooftop dinner.

Want to add Abu Dhabi?

The Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque and the Louvre Abu Dhabi are each extraordinary in their own right. We can extend your trip by a day or two to include them — just let us know.
