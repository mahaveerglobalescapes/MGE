About Us

Built from a Love
of Real Travel

Mahaveer Global Escapes was born from a simple frustration — that most travel feels copy-pasted. We decided to do it differently, from day one.

Why We Started

The Frustration That
Became a Mission

We've all experienced it — the pre-packaged itinerary that doesn't quite fit. The hotel that was "best in category" but nothing like what you actually wanted. The group tour where you spent half your time waiting around.

Mahaveer Global Escapes started because we believed travel deserved better. Not better in terms of luxury or price — but better in terms of fit. A trip that actually matches the traveller.

So we built something different. A travel service where the planning starts with a real conversation — where we ask questions, understand who you are, and then build a journey around that.

13+

Journeys built from scratch since we started — each one completely different, shaped entirely by the traveller who asked.

"The world is large. Cookie-cutter trips are a waste of the limited days we get to see it."

How We Work

Our Approach

01

We Listen First

Before a single destination is suggested, we understand who you are. Your travel style, your budget, your worries, your dreams. Most agencies skip this entirely. It's where we begin.

02

We Build From Scratch

No templates. No pulled-from-shelf itineraries. We design your journey fresh — every hotel choice, every activity, every connection — based entirely on what you told us.

03

We Stay With You

When you travel, we're still there. Not in spirit — actually there, on call, ready to help with anything that comes up. Because the journey doesn't end when you board your flight.

What We Believe In

Our Values

🎯

Personalisation Over Package

Every traveller is different. We refuse to treat two people the same just because their budget or destination matches. Your journey is yours alone.

🤝

Honesty Over Upselling

We recommend what fits — not what earns us more. If a budget option is better for you, that's what we'll suggest. Your trust matters more than our margin.

📞

Availability Over Automation

You'll always reach a real person when you reach us. No bots, no delayed responses. We are your travel partner — and partners answer when called.

✨

Detail Over Convenience

The difference between a good trip and a great one is often in the small things. We sweat the details because we know they're what you'll remember most.
